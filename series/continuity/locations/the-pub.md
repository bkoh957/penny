# The Pub

Where the town drinks, argues, and accidentally confesses.

**In Book 01.** The informal gathering for Neil (ch 26) is held here — Beryl organises it, which is how you know it is official. People say things about Neil more complicated than the first time. Faye cries. Cobber says something that sounds like nonsense and turns out not to be. Artie Selwood sits in the corner watching Meg.

A key location for community set-pieces and the kind of conversation that happens when people feel they are off the record.
