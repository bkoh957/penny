# The Op-Shop / General Store

Run by Dot and Glad. The town's unofficial surveillance headquarters.

The unrelated lookalikes miss nothing that happens on the main street. The op-shop location gives them an unobstructed view of the main street, behind a display of donated crockery that functions as a one-way screen. Assessment of newcomers is delivered from behind this screen.

**Fair-play function.** A reliable source of clues delivered through comic, contradictory testimony — Dot and Glad agree on the large facts and diverge on every detail.
