# The Lighthouse

Sits on the headland. Artie Selwood's domain — he is the retired keeper.

Atmosphere, history, and a bottomless well of old local secrets. The coast visible from the lighthouse is the wreck-strewn stretch where the town's founding ambiguity (rescue or plunder?) played out.

**In Book 01.** Artie corners Meg here in ch 16 and tells her, very carefully, that he once knew a woman who saw the way Meg sees. He does not say more. Changes the subject to the 1878 wreck of the Loch Ard, which he narrates in exhausting detail. The lighthouse visit is the first hint of the gift's origin mystery.

**Series role.** Artie's record — thirty years of keeping something — is connected to the lighthouse. Do not resolve what it is in Book 01.
