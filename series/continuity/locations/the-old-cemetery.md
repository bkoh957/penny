# The Old Cemetery

Up the hill behind town. Graves of unidentified shipwrecked sailors — the town's long memory made literal.

The location of Calloway's buried bench skeleton (series seed; not resolved in Book 01). The cemetery embeds the town's wreck-and-salvage past and its moral ambiguity: who was buried here and why, and how much of what the founding families did should stay buried.

A recurring atmospheric location. The key locations in the outline's Solution block include the old cemetery, suggesting a scene — likely early, to establish tone — without disclosing anything solution-specific.
