# The Bakery

Run by Faye. Warm, floury heart of the town gossip network. The natural exposition engine: townsfolk pass through, news flows in and out.

Faye delivers sourdough and social history simultaneously. The bakery is where Meg learns the town's hidden map of feuds and history.

The jam feud is fought from this location annually; the agricultural-show judge's arrival sets the whole establishment on edge.

Ch 10 note: the car park behind the bakery is where Beryl and Neil were overheard arguing the week before the murder — a fact Faye knows and will tell Meg if given the chance.
