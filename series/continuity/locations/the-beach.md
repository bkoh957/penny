# The Beach

Wide, dramatic, often empty off-season. Cobber's permanent station — he is there every dawn, and still there at noon.

**In Book 01.** Maggie nods at Cobber on the beach in ch 2; he is established as a permanent feature of the dawn landscape. His sighting (a car, a figure, an approximate time near Neil's) is assembled into the timeline in ch 20.

The beach at dawn is also one of the key locations listed in the outline's Solution block — a scene location for investigation or aftermath, tonal and atmospheric.
