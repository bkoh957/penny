# The Wellness Retreat

Saffron's operation. Green juice, crystals, yoga, unsolicited energy readings. Too fancy; the locals resent it. The town-versus-blow-in tension in a building.

May be unlicensed. Saffron's financial situation (quietly broke) means the retreat's future is uncertain.

The retreat is not a primary scene location in Book 01; Saffron's presence in scenes is more often in public town spaces (she sweeps through town, drops flyers, appears at the market). The retreat exists as context for her character and motive.
