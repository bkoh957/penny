# Thread: A — Murder

A beloved GP is found dead. The investigation — driven by Maggie's Migraine Sight and the friction of Sergeant Pruitt's scepticism — is the A-plot of Book 01. The mystery is one of misread mercy: someone killed the doctor believing they were avenging a crime. The truth surfaces in three movements: Neil mourned as saint (chs 1–8), exposed as a possible sinner when his old records surface (chs 9–17), then understood as a person who acted from love (chs 18–26).
