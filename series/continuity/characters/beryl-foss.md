# Beryl Foss

**Role:** Social gatekeeper; CWA chair; comic antagonist (affectionate). First appears: ch 2.

Runs the town's social calendar with an iron fist and a clipboard. Every fundraiser, market, and funeral runs through her. She is the institutional power of the town — to be accepted in Pelican's Crook is to be accepted by Beryl. Did Not Approve of the newcomer.

**Red herring role (chs 10–14).** She was overheard arguing with Neil in the car park behind the bakery; her manner under questioning goes very still. Her secret: Neil gave her a diagnosis she has told nobody, and the argument was her demanding he keep it out of her file and his refusing, gently, because he could not in good conscience. The iron matriarch is mortal and does not want the town to know. Cleared ch 14.

**Arc.** Thaws from obstacle to fierce, prickly ally across the series. Her illness humanises her without diminishing her authority.

**Note.** Her events (markets, fundraisers, hall clean-ups) provide the warm community set-pieces every book needs — and a stage for Meg's gift to misfire publicly.
