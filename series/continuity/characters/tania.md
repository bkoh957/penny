# Tania

**Role:** Faye's bakery staff member; relaxed, iconic, unflappable bakery-life texture. First mentioned: ch 2.

Tania works for Faye at the bakery and minds the ovens. Faye may claim Tania panics, but the truth is the opposite: Tania seems casual, even slow, because she is already three moves ahead. The ovens obey her. The queue does not frighten her. Customers who make Faye anxious get handled by Tania with a calm look, a paper bag and one devastatingly mild sentence.

She is relaxed, local-iconic and quietly competent — the person Maggie initially underestimates because she looks too calm to be managing anything, then realises relaxed is not the same as careless. She can tell bread is ready by smell and knows which regular is buying bread, which is buying news, and which is pretending to do one while doing the other.

**Function.** Makes Faye's bakery feel alive and gives Faye a warmer comic counterweight. Not a suspect, not a major subplot, but useful recurring cozy texture.
