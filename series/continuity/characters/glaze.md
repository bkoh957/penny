# Glaze

**Role:** Studio cat. Cosy mascot. Emotional anchor. First appears: ch 1.

A scruffy ginger tom of uncertain allegiance who walked in through The Wheelhouse's open door in ch 1, inspected the kiln, and sat on Maggie's good apron. She did not throw him out. He is now permanent. Named for the glaze he is permanently dusted in; considers the kiln his personal heater.

Obligatory cosy companion — comic relief and a soft place for Maggie (and the reader) to land after hard scenes. Occupies neutral territory. Occasionally, conveniently, draws attention to the right corner of a room.

**Recurring beat.** He is on the bonnet of Maggie's car in the surf club car park when she sits on the kerb after the Saffron collapse (ch 17). Neutral territory between Maggie and Cal in the ch 28 studio conversation.

## Knowledge state
*As of ch-01 end.* Has claimed The Wheelhouse as territory; inspected the kiln; claimed Maggie's good apron. Maggie did not remove him.

## Established facts
- Large, disreputable-looking ginger tom; one ear stands straight, one "had given up."
- Clay dust worn so thoroughly "it had simply become part of him."
- Entered The Wheelhouse through the open door in ch-01 with the air of having been there before; Maggie assumed he probably had.
- Inspected the kiln: walked around it twice, applied one paw briefly to the door seal, then sat with tail curled in measured satisfaction.
- Settled on Maggie's good apron (folded on top of the second box); rearranged himself, tucked his chin down, and closed his eyes "with an air of finality." She did not move him.
