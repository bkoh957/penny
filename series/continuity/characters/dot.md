# Dot

**Role:** One half of the op-shop surveillance duo. First appears: ch 2.

Runs the op-shop / general store with her sister Glad. Elderly; the town's unofficial CCTV. Dot and Glad miss nothing that happens on the main street. The catch: they have not agreed on a single fact since 1987. Their testimony agrees on the large facts and diverges on every detail (the car was dark blue — Dot; dark green — Glad).

**In Book 01:** Their contradictory account of a car parked briefly outside Neil's is a necessary clue (ch 11). The real fact buried beneath their disagreement: a car, unusual for that street at that hour. The colour dispute is Dot/Glad at their finest — the truth requires adjudication.

**Note.** Dot and Glad are best written as a unit: they finish each other's sentences and are never apart.
