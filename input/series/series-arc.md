## Unassigned ideas
*Premises without a book number yet. Slot into numbered books when the shape gets clearer.*

- **Tommy comes to town** — Maggie's ex-husband arrives in Pelican's Crook and ends up a murder suspect who looks very guilty.
- **Cal accused** — Cal is accused of murder; Maggie has to investigate to clear him (or confront what she finds).
- **Maggie's parents visit** — her parents come to Pelican's Crook; community backdrop, personal pressure, likely ties into the c-internal or gift thread.
- **The vanished potter** — The previous Wheelhouse potter did not simply leave town; they vanished. When their family arrives expecting to find them still at The Wheelhouse and instead finds Maggie, the old disappearance becomes a murder investigation — and Maggie, now occupying the dead potter's studio and life-shaped space, becomes one of the main suspects.
